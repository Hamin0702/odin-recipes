Description

Paella is a traditional Spanish rice dish originating from Valencia. It's typically made with saffron-seasoned rice, a mix of seafood or meats, vegetables, and cooked in a wide, shallow pan. It's colorful, aromatic, and perfect for gatherings.

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tomato, grated or finely chopped
  • Salt to taste

Protein:

  • 8 large shrimp or prawns
  • 8 mussels or clams (scrubbed)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken (optional)
  • 1 chorizo sausage, sliced (optional)

Vegetables:

  • 1/2 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, sliced
  • Lemon wedges (for garnish)
  • Chopped parsley (for garnish)
